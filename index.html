About Prozo-Life

Prozo-Life is a dynamic platform built to empower student developers by connecting them with real-world clients. We aim to create a space where learning meets practical experience. Whether you're a first-year student looking to enhance your skills or a final-year developer ready for industry-level exposure, Prozo-Life provides the right opportunities.

How We Work

We bring together aspiring developers and clients through a structured workflow:

  1. Clients submit their project requirements.
  2. We assign a team of vetted student developers based on skills.
  3. Regular updates and communication are maintained throughout the project.
  4. Final delivery with client feedback and certification for developers.
